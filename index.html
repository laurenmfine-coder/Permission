About Lauren

I'm the proof
of concept.

I built a medical practice, left it. Became a medical educator, then founded an EdTech platform. Along the way I became a coach, gave a TEDx talk, and started writing about what it actually takes to change.

I'm not a coach who talks about transitions from the sidelines. I've lived every pivot I guide others through.
