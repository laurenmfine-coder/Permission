About Lauren

I'm the proof
of concept.

I built a medical practice, left it. Became a medical educator, then founded an EdTech platform. Then I became a coach, gave a TEDx talk, and started writing about what it actually takes to change.

What ties it all together: each pivot was a decision about what mattered, not just what was possible. That is the work I help others do now. Clarify the inputs. Map the trade-offs. Build the story that lets you choose, and live with the choice.

I'm not a coach who talks about transitions from the sidelines. I've lived every pivot I guide others through.
